# Prompt — ugc-multi-location A-roll clip (`kling3_0`, sound ON)

Same template as ugc-single (`formats/ugc-single/prompts/a-roll-clip.md`) — pose-match
the location's extract, voice tag verbatim, exact dialogue, continuity close. Two
location-specific notes:

- **Walking beats** may use the slight-handheld camera ("a slight, natural handheld
  drift as she walks") — still NO zoom/pan/dolly.
- **Restate the exact wardrobe** for this location every clip (the anti-drift anchor).

See the ugc-single worked example; apply per location.

## Worked example (walking, PROBLEM beat)
> Begin on the exact medium shot of `<persona>` from the reference walking along a sunny
> sidewalk, matching the reference framing, wearing `<the exact wardrobe for this
> location>`, the street and warm daylight consistent. The camera holds a slight,
> natural handheld drift as she walks, no other movement. She speaks to the camera in
> `<voice tag verbatim>`, wry and relatable, as a matter-of-fact STATEMENT with falling
> intonation: "I used to just doom-scroll on every commute." A small self-aware shrug.
> Identity, wardrobe, lighting, and background completely consistent — a seamless single take.
