# Prompt — interview answer clip (`kling3_0`, sound ON)

Start frame = that interviewee's extract. They answer the off-camera question. Voice tag
verbatim (per interviewee), handheld street energy, continuity close. INTERVIEWER lines
are handled as audio (over a mic/establishing cutaway) — not a rendered interviewer face
in POV v1.

## Template
> Begin on the handheld street shot of `<interviewee>` from the reference, a mic just in
> the lower frame, reacting to an off-camera question in `<their voice tag verbatim>`,
> `<attitude: curious / surprised / skeptical-then-sold>`: "`<answer, exact words>`".
> `<Candid reaction: a laugh, a "no way" head-tilt, glancing to the off-camera
> interviewer>`. Handheld drift, blurred passersby, wardrobe + lighting consistent — a
> seamless single take.

## Worked example (PERSON2 cameo, converts)
> Begin on the handheld street shot of PERSON2 from the reference, mic entering the
> lower frame, reacting to the off-camera pitch in `<their voice tag verbatim>`,
> instantly sold: "Okay I'm downloading that right now, hold on." A grin, already
> pulling out a Black Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra, its bright screen facing the camera
> showing the Mode Earn App store page exactly as in the reference. Handheld drift,
> blurred passersby, wardrobe + lighting consistent — a seamless single take.
