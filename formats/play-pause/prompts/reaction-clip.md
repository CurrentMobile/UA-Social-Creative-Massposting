# Prompt — play-pause reaction clip (`kling3_0`, sound ON)

Start frame = the watching still. The reactor turns from their device to camera and
talks (this plays while the PiP is frozen). Voice tag verbatim; camera static;
continuity close.

## Template
> Begin on the angled front view of `<persona>` from the reference, `<glancing at their
> device, then>` turning to look directly into the lens. They react in `<voice tag
> verbatim>`, `<reaction attitude: surprised / skeptical-then-convinced / amused>`:
> "`<turn dialogue, exact words>`". `<A natural reaction gesture: raised eyebrow,
> hand-to-chin, pointing at the corner where the clip was>`. Wardrobe, lighting, and
> background completely consistent, camera completely static — a seamless single take.

For "watch" beats (reactor quiet, PiP playing), either reuse the still as a hold or
generate a short neutral watching clip (eyes on device, subtle reactions, no speech).

## Worked example
> Begin on the angled front view of the reactor from the reference at their desk,
> glancing at the laptop then turning to the lens. They react in `<voice tag verbatim>`,
> surprised and skeptical: "Wait — pause. It's literally rewarding her for playing
> music?" A raised eyebrow and a small disbelieving head-tilt, pointing back at the
> corner. Wardrobe, lighting, and background completely consistent, camera static — a
> seamless single take.
