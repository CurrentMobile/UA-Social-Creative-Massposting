# Prompt — lofi-text ambient loop clip (`kling3_0`, sound OFF, 4-6s)

Start frame = the scene still. The whole point is a SEAMLESS loop: prompt only gentle,
cyclic ambient motion and make the frame look the same at start and end. Camera fully
static (or the faintest drift). No zooms, no pans, no big moves.

## Template

> `<Ambient cyclic motion tied to the scene: rain streaking down the glass / steam
> curling slowly upward / fairy lights softly twinkling / a thumb slowly scrolling the
> screen / curtains breathing gently>`, `<the phone screen's glow subtly pulses>`.
> Everything drifts calmly and the frame looks the same at the start and end of the
> loop. The camera holds completely static. Cozy, hypnotic, seamless.

Loop-point rule (LOCKED in the SOP): whatever moves must be back near its starting
position by the end. Keep motions small and continuous, never a one-way action that
leaves the frame changed.

## Worked example (coffee-shop table)

> Steam curls slowly and continuously upward from the coffee cup, the window light
> flickers faintly as blurred people drift past outside, and the phone screen's glow
> gently pulses; all motion is soft and cyclic and the frame looks identical at the
> start and end of the loop. The camera holds completely static. Calm, warm, seamless.
