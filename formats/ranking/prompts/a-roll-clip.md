# Prompt — ranking A-roll clip (`kling3_0`, sound ON)

Same template as ugc-single (`formats/ugc-single/prompts/a-roll-clip.md`) — pose-match,
voice tag verbatim, exact rank line, continuity close. The ranking difference is the
**escalating delivery** and the **phone reveal gated to rank 1**.

## Delivery arc
- Low ranks (5-3): measured, slightly dismissive, matter-of-fact ("it's fine, but…").
- Rank 2: warming up, a little impressed.
- Rank 1: peak energy, genuine excitement, phone comes up showing the app.
- Keep statements declarative (firm period + "delivers as a matter-of-fact STATEMENT
  with falling intonation, NOT a question").

## Worked example (RANK 1 reveal, ~12 words, 5s)
> Begin on the exact medium close-up of `<persona>` from the reference, looking into
> the lens, matching the reference pose, wearing `<exact wardrobe>`, `<environment>`
> and lighting completely unchanged. The camera remains completely static. Her energy
> lifts with genuine excitement as she speaks in `<voice tag verbatim>`, delivering as
> an emphatic STATEMENT with a rising-then-settling, confident falling intonation:
> "Number one, and it's not close — the Mode Earn App." As she says the name she brings
> up a Black Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra, its bright screen facing the camera clearly
> showing the Mode Earn App home screen exactly as in the reference image, a delighted
> grin. Lighting, wardrobe, and background completely consistent — a seamless single take.

Low-rank example (RANK 4): same template, "mildly unimpressed, a small shrug", no phone.
