# Prompt — ugc-single A-roll clip (`kling3_0`, sound ON)

First frame = the chunk's `img-<N>.png`. Duration = the chunk's
`recommended_duration_s`. `mode=pro`, `sound=on`, aspect 9:16. Paste the persona's
`voice-tag.md` verbatim into the voice slot every time.

## Template

> Begin on the exact `<shot type: frontal close-up / medium close-up / medium /
> wide-medium>` of `<character incl age + identity>` looking directly into the lens,
> matching the reference pose precisely `<pose detail>`, wearing `<exact wardrobe>`.
> `<Environment>` with `<lighting>` remains completely unchanged. The camera `<is
> completely static with no movement | introduces a slight, natural handheld drift>`
> throughout the shot. She begins speaking directly to the camera in `<VOICE TAG
> pasted verbatim>`, delivering with `<pacing from the voice tag>`:
> "`<DIALOGUE CHUNK — exact words>`". As she speaks, `<one natural gesture/action tied
> to the line, in the persona's personality>`. Her expression stays `<on-character:
> conversational / sassy / wry / hype>`. The shot resolves with `<ending pose>`,
> lighting, wardrobe, and all background details staying completely consistent
> throughout — a seamless single take.

Rules (see `generate_assets.md` personality table + `camera-rules.md` intonation note):
- Perform in the persona's personality — never flat/generic; write gestures in.
- Statements end with a firm period + "delivers the line as a calm, matter-of-fact
  STATEMENT with a settled, declarative, falling intonation, NOT a question."
- Phone in frame ⇒ phone visibility grammar clause 1 + front prop ref + app UI ref.

## Worked example (chunk 1, ~10 words, duration 4s)

> Begin on the exact frontal medium close-up of the 60-something woman looking directly
> into the lens, matching her relaxed reference pose with hands resting in her lap,
> wearing a soft cardigan over a blouse, with warm natural window light and a cozy
> living room behind her remaining completely unchanged. The camera remains completely
> static with no movement. She begins speaking directly to the camera in a warm,
> soothing, trustworthy tone with a slightly raspy, rich lower register and slow,
> deliberate pacing: "I'm retired… the last thing I wanted was another job." As she
> speaks she gives a small, knowing shake of the head and a gentle wave-off gesture,
> her expression candid and warm, settling into a relaxed posture as the line
> concludes — lighting, wardrobe, and background completely consistent, a seamless
> single take.
