# Prompts — static-ad base visuals (`gpt_image_2`)

One base per copy variation, chosen by the intake `sub_format`. Leave clean negative
space / flat plates for composited text (don't bake headline text — it warps). Phone in
frame ⇒ phone visibility grammar (screen to viewer, real UI, `s22-ultra-front.png` +
app-UI still refs). Persona subject ⇒ pass `base-character.png`. Inject guardrails
first.

## lifestyle-photo
> A warm candid lifestyle photo of `<persona/subject>` `<real moment>`, holding a Black
> Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra with its bright screen facing the camera clearly showing the
> <app> home screen exactly as in the reference image, natural relaxed smile. Soft
> natural light, shallow DOF, authentic UGC feel, clean negative space at the `<top/
> bottom>` for a headline. Photorealistic.

## feature-callout
> A clean studio hero shot of a Black Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra floating at a slight
> angle on a `<brand-color>` gradient, its bright screen facing the camera clearly
> showing the <app> `<feature>` screen exactly as in the reference, soft product
> lighting + reflection, generous negative space for a headline and a callout label.
> Photorealistic, crisp, ad-ready.
