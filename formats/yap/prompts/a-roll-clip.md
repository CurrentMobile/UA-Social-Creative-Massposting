# Prompt — yap A-roll clip (`kling3_0`, sound ON)

Same template as ugc-single (`formats/ugc-single/prompts/a-roll-clip.md`) with three
yap-specific shifts: CLOSE selfie framing, FAST animated delivery, and a bit more
handheld selfie drift allowed (still no zoom/pan/dolly). Voice tag verbatim but paced
fast; continuity close.

## Worked example (HOOK)
> Begin on the close selfie-angle shot of `<persona>` from the reference, face filling
> the frame, direct eye contact, wearing `<casual wardrobe>`, `<casual setting>` behind
> slightly out of focus. The camera has a slight, natural handheld selfie drift. They
> talk FAST and animated in `<voice tag verbatim>`, urgent "I have to tell you this"
> energy: "okay I need to talk about this because I genuinely thought it was fake." Big
> expressive eyebrows, leaning toward the lens, a quick disbelieving hand flick.
> Wardrobe, lighting, and background consistent — a seamless single take.

Keep every clip high-energy; the format dies if the delivery is flat.
